FAQ

Before You Buy a Ceiling Fan

The questions we hear most often are about noise, installation, the room to equip and trust in the brand.

Yes. ORAE models use a DC motor with multiple speeds, so you can keep a low, steady speed in a bedroom. For a nursery or night-time use, choose a low speed and a well-balanced installation.

A ceiling fan does not cool the air like air conditioning, but it greatly improves perceived comfort by keeping air moving. Useful in a bedroom, a living room, a guest house or a holiday rental.

You need a power supply at the ceiling, a mount suited to the surface and sufficient height. For a safe installation, ORAE recommends having the fan fitted by a competent person or an electrician.

The remote is enough for everyday use. The Tuya app adds control from your phone: speed, timer and lighting on LED models, at home or away depending on your Wi-Fi setup.
